I am a final-year PhD student in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Professor Liyao Xiang. I obtained my B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) in 2020 from the University of Michigan - Shanghai Jiao Tong University Joint Institute.

My current research focuses on the intersection of computer security and machine learning. I work towards identifying privacy risks in machine learning systems and developing practical countermeasures.
